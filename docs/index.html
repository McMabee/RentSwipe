How it works

For tenants

Find and compare student rentals quickly. Listings that have been third‑party verified so you can trust each posting is legitimate. Use filters to narrow results by price, location, amenities and commute time. Save favourites, view detailed photos and landlord profiles, and connect directly via in‑app messaging. Our comparison tools surface key differences (rent, utilities, deposit, lease length) so you can make confident choices faster.

For landlords

Fill your rooms and houses faster by reaching a large pool of local students. Post listings, manage availability, and screen applicants through our platform. RentSwipe highlights that tenants are students in the area, helping you find reliable occupants quickly. Communicate with applicants and tenants directly in‑app and track conversations from interest through move‑in.

Built-in, moderated communication

Tenants and landlords can easily communicate within the app. When disputes arise, for example, cleanliness concerns or repair requests, our moderated chat lets both sides raise issues and work toward a resolution with oversight. Conversations are logged, and moderation helps ensure problems are handled promptly and ethically, protecting both parties and keeping living spaces well maintained.
